== Description ==
= Subscribe students to your list upon enrollment =
Are you looking to subscribe your students to your email list? It's now possible with the ConvertKit add-on for WP Courseware. Upon enrollment, you can subscribe your students to one of the following [ConvertKit](https://flyplugins.com/convertkit) elements:

* forms
* sequences
* tags

= Create an online course lead magnet with webhooks =
Webhooks are a super powerful method to take advantage of automation that will automatically enroll a student into a course when one of the following behaviors occur:

* New Subscriber is added
* Someone subscribes to a form
* Someone is added to a sequence
* Someone is tagged

Once you configure your ConvertKit API Key and Secret you can begin configuring your courses.

== Installation ==

1. Go in to your WordPress Admin Panel.
2. Navigate to Plugins &rarr; Add New and click upload.
3. Browse for the wpcw-convertkit.zip file and click upload.
4. Click Activate.
